Blood Pressure Guidelines

Understanding your blood pressure readings

Normal

Less than 120/80 mmHg

Maintain your healthy lifestyle with regular exercise and balanced diet.

Elevated

120-129/<80 mmHg

Implement lifestyle changes to prevent hypertension development.

Stage 1 Hypertension

130-139/80-89 mmHg

Consult with healthcare provider. Medication may be needed.

Stage 2 Hypertension

140/90 mmHg or higher

Seek immediate medical attention and treatment.

Hypertension Prevention Tips

Daily habits to maintain healthy blood pressure

Exercise Regularly

Aim for 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity per week

Healthy Eating

Follow DASH diet - rich in vegetables, fruits, lean proteins

Reduce Sodium

Limit salt intake to less than 2,300 mg per day

Quality Sleep

Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep every night

Stress Management

Practice meditation, yoga, or relaxation techniques

Avoid Harmful Habits

Limit alcohol and avoid smoking completely
